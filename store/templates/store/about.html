About Indigo

A collective of passionate artisans creating handmade pottery, wood decorations, and jewelry with love and tradition.

Who We Are

The brain child of Addy Heart, Indigo is a small ensemble of artisans dedicated to preserving the beauty of handmade crafts. From earthy pottery to intricate wood decorations and delicate earrings and necklaces, each piece tells a story rooted in tradition and creativity.

Based in our cozy workshop, we blend timeless techniques with modern design, offering you pieces that are both personal and unique.

Come Visit Us

Our studio is open by appointment most weekends. We love showing people how things are made, and there's usually coffee brewing.
